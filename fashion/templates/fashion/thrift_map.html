Thrift Map

Goodwill

This thrift store is within walking distance from campus. It is a go to thrifting spot for many Novos.

GDC Free Store

This thrift store is actually on campus ad everything is Free! Stop by the GDC and see if there are any items you wanna grab or leave behind for others.

Canned Ham Vintage

This is a great vintage spot with tons of unique pieces. It is a little pricier, but totally worth the extra dollars!

SPARCC Treasure Chest

Designer clothing, jewelry, furniture, home accessories, antiques & collectibles, all at bargain prices!

The Exchange Inc.

Consignment store for furniture, decor, clothing, bags & shoes, with proceeds benefitting the arts.

The Blue Heirloom Vintage

Vintage resale store with over 4K square feet of retro, vintage, mid-century and antique items and collectibles.

Mission Thrift Sarasota

Mission Thrift is a non profit thrift store with over 10,000 square feet of gently used clothes, furniture, sporting goods and more! Proceeds benefit children's ministries in our communities!

Sunshine Thrift Stores of Bradenton, Inc.

Sunshine is a Florida based for-profit corporation formed in 1982 to assist local charitable organizations with their fundraising efforts. They have amazing thrifting options and is our favorite pick in Sarasota!
